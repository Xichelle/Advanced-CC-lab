It is probably a love written in the genes from the very beginning,

from the Neolithic village of Gligal in the Jordan Valley

when people were attracted to figs and planted them,

as if human civilization and figs had been quietly linked.

Figs, with their full fruits,

steadily satisfied people's desire for sweetness since ancient times,

and also filled my empty childhood.
