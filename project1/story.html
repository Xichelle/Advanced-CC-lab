I have seen a world where people no longer feel joy and pain for life
Work became the meaning of life,
doing the same thing over and over again
Build white graves in paper
and breathe rust-smelling poison by the assembly line,
step by step into the foreseeable tomorrow and beyond
An old man looked up at me and asked:
"Young man, Why don't you go to work?"
I didn't talk. I just looked at him
As if my eyes were a lens that could capture the past, present, and future
